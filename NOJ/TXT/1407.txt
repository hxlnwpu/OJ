    1407.the peanut
   
    时限：1000ms 内存限制：10000K  总时限：3000ms
   
    描述
   
    五一劳动节假期，Tom和Jerry在仓库散步的时候又发现了一堆花生米。Tom制定分花生米规则如下：
    
    1、Tom和Jerry轮流从堆中取出k粒花生米吃掉，k可以是1，3，4中的任意一个数字；
    
    2、为显示规则的公平性，Jerry可以选择先取或者后取。
    
    谁取到最后一粒花生米谁胜。
    
    Jerry当然希望自己获得胜利。请计算，Jerry为了达到目的应该先取还是后取。
    
    May Day, Tom and Jerry is walking in the warehouse and found a bunch of peanuts. Tom make rules as follows:
    
    1. Tom and jerry take k peanut from the heap in turn, k can be any of a number in 1,3,4;
    
    2. To show the fairness of rules, Jerry can choose to take it first or second.
    
    Who take the last one will win this game.
    
    Jerry, of course, want to win. Calculation, Jerry order to achieve their goals should take it first or not.
   
    输入
   
    输入是一个正整数n，代表花生米的数量。
    
    Input a positive integer n, representative the quantity of peanuts.
   
    输出
   
    如果Jerry决定先取，则输出1，否则输出0。
    
    If Jerry decided to take it first output 1, otherwise output 0.
   
    输入样例
   
    3
    
    输出样例
   
    1
