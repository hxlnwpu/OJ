    1404.Digital Split
   
    时限：1000ms 内存限制：10000K  总时限：3000ms
   
    描述
   
    Input a three-digit number,and output the numbe of it’s respective positions seperately.Each digit occupies one line only.
   
    输入
   
    Input a three-digit positive number
   
    输出
   
    Output the numbe of it’s respective positions seperately and Sequentially
   
    输入样例
   
    123
   
    输出样例
   
    1
    
    2
    
    3
